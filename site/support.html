Frequently asked questions

Why does the export control not appear?

Make sure the current page is an active ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini conversation, then refresh the tab. If it still does not appear, confirm the extension is enabled and open AI Chat Export from the browser toolbar.

Why can a long conversation fail to export?

Very long conversations may contain unloaded messages, oversized code blocks, cross-origin images, or content that exceeds browser rendering limits. Scroll the conversation to load it fully, then try Word or Markdown for the most reliable result.

How do I sync Pro access?

Use the same email for checkout and extension login. Then refresh the AI page or use restore purchase inside the extension. If access still does not appear, send the purchase email and order details to support.

Are exported files uploaded to ChatVault?

No. PDF, Docs, MD and More files are generated locally in your browser. Account and payment services handle login, email, user ID, and subscription status, not the chat body.

How is AI Chat Export different from ChatVault AI?

AI Chat Export focuses on exporting the current conversation into polished local files. ChatVault AI focuses on longer-term organization, folders, bookmarks, title sync, and knowledge management. The products can share the same account and Pro entitlement.

Helpful details to include

  • The platform where the issue happened: ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.
  • The export output type, such as PDF, Docs, MD and More.
  • Your browser version and AI Chat Export extension version.
  • The exact error message or a screenshot of the export health check.
  • Whether the conversation includes images, long code blocks, tables, or very long answers.
