Privacy is part of the workflow.

The extension reads the current page only when you ask it to export. Chat message bodies are not sent to ChatVault servers for conversion.

  • PDF, Docs, MD and More files are generated locally in the browser.
  • Account services handle login, email, user ID, and subscription status.
  • Payment processing is handled by Paddle. Card details are not stored by ChatVault.
  • The extension does not collect platform cookies, passwords, access tokens, or refresh tokens.
  • Images may be fetched from the original AI platform or its image CDN to preserve the exported file.
