Privacy Policy

AI Chat Export is a privacy-first browser extension for exporting current ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini conversations to local files. We keep conversion work in the browser whenever possible and limit account and payment data to what is needed for login and Pro access.

Data we process

When you choose to export, the extension reads the selected conversation content from the current page so it can generate a file locally. Depending on your settings, exported content may include messages, AI replies, code blocks, tables, images, page title, original URL, platform name, and export time.

When you sign in or purchase Pro, we process account email, user ID, login session data, subscription status, plan type, transaction state returned by the payment provider, and minimal technical logs needed to keep access working.

Local generation

  • PDF, Docs, MD and More files are generated in your browser.
  • Chat message bodies are not sent to ChatVault servers, Supabase, or Paddle for file conversion.
  • The extension may use your active platform session cookies or access token locally in your browser to fetch conversation history or images you choose to export. These platform credentials are not stored on or sent to ChatVault servers.
  • We do not collect platform passwords or refresh tokens.
  • Images may be fetched from the original AI platform or its image CDN so they can appear in the exported file.

Local storage

The extension may store export preferences, theme settings, daily free-limit counters, login state cache, and local error state in your browser. This helps the export workflow stay stable without giving us access to chat bodies.

Accounts, subscriptions, and payments

Pro access is verified through account and payment services. Payments may be processed by Paddle or another payment provider. We do not store card numbers or complete payment credentials. We keep the minimum subscription records needed to sync Pro access to the extension.

AI Chat Export's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs follows the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Analytics and logs

We may record non-content events such as extension version, export format, export success or failure, error type, and subscription refresh result. Prompts, AI replies, and exported file bodies are not written into analytics events.

Data sharing

We do not sell user data. We may share necessary data with infrastructure, authentication, payment, and email service providers only to operate the service.

Deletion and account requests

You can contact chatvaultaisupport@gmail.com to request account data deletion, subscription lookup, or privacy support. Browser-local preferences and counters can be removed by uninstalling the extension or clearing extension data.

Children's privacy

AI Chat Export is not directed to children. If you believe we accidentally collected personal information from a child, contact us so we can remove it.

Policy updates

We may update this policy as the product, laws, or compliance requirements change. Important changes will be noted on the website or inside the extension. Continued use of AI Chat Export means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, and security feedback can be sent to chatvaultaisupport@gmail.com.
